Video Editing

Video editing is the process of manipulating and rearranging video shots. It is used to structure and present all video information, including films and television shows, video advertisements and video essays. Video editing has been dramatically simplified by the use of non-linear editing systems (NLE). This system allows for almost any number of video clips to be layered in a predefined or free-form manner, using a variety of customizable video transitions, filters, and effects.

Crop Disease detection

Agriculture plays a vital role in sustaining economies and ensuring food security. Timely detection of crop diseases and accurate yield prediction are essential for maximizing agricultural productivity and minimizing losses. Traditional methods for these tasks are often labor-intensive and less precise. This web application leverages Graph Convolutional Networks (GCNs) to enhance crop disease detection and yield prediction. GCNs, a type of neural network adept at handling graph-structured data, are utilized to analyze relationships between various data points, such as images, environmental conditions, and historical yield data. For disease detection, images and metadata are represented as graph nodes, with edges capturing spatial and feature-based relationships, allowing the GCN to accurately identify disease symptoms.

Fake Image Detection

Fake image detection is increasingly critical in a digital age where manipulated images can easily mislead audiences and spread misinformation. This project leverages machine learning techniques to develop a robust fake image detection system. By training convolutional neural networks (CNNs) on large datasets of authentic and manipulated images, the system learns to identify subtle inconsistencies and artifacts indicative of tampering. The model utilizes advanced image processing techniques to analyze pixel-level features and extract patterns that differentiate real images from fake ones. This detection system provides a valuable tool for verifying image authenticity, enhancing security, and maintaining the integrity of digital media across various platforms.
